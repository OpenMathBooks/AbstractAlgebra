(1) We must show that

\begin{equation*} h \circ (g \circ f) = (h \circ g) \circ f\text{.} \end{equation*}

For \(a \in A\) we have

\begin{align*} (h \circ (g \circ f))(a) & = h((g \circ f)(a))\\ & = h(g(f(a)))\\ & = (h \circ g)(f(a))\\ & = ((h \circ g) \circ f)(a)\text{.} \end{align*}
