Consider the two polynomials \(p(x) = 5x^2 + 3x + 6\) and \(q(x) = 4x^2 - x + 9\text{.}\)

  1. Compute \(p(x)+q(x)\) and \(p(x)q(x)\) in the ring \(\Z[x]\text{.}\)

  2. Compute \(p(x)+q(x)\) and \(p(x)q(x)\) in the ring \(\R[x]\text{.}\)

  3. Compute \(p(x)+q(x)\) and \(p(x)q(x)\) in the ring \(\Z_{12}[x]\text{.}\)
