Solution 6.7.6.1.

Let's use the Euclidean algorithm:

\begin{align*} x^{24} - 1 = \amp x^9(x^{15} - 1) + x^9 - 1 \\ x^{15} - 1 = \amp x^6(x^{9} - 1) + x^6 - 1 \\ x^{9} - 1 = \amp x^3(x^{6} - 1) + x^3 - 1 \\ x^{6} - 1 = \amp (x^3+1)(x^{3} - 1) + 0 \end{align*}

Thus the gcd is \(x^3-1\text{.}\)

Now work backwards:

\begin{align*} x^3 - 1 = \amp [x^9-1] - x^3[x^6-1] \\ x^3 - 1 = \amp [x^9-1] - x^3([x^{15}-1] - x^6[x^9-1]) \\ = \amp -x^3[x^{15}-1] + (x^9+1)[x^9-1] \\ x^3 - 1 = \amp -x^3[x^{15}-1] + (x^9+1)([x^{24} - 1] - x^9[x^{15}-1] ) \\ = \amp (x^9+1)[x^{24}-1] - (x^{18}+x^9 +x^3)[x^{15}-1] \end{align*}
