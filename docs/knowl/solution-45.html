Solution 6.2.1.1.

The coefficients must be either 0 or 1. The coefficient of \(x^2\) must be 1, otherwise the degree would be less than 2. For each of the other terms, we could have coefficient 0 or 1, so there are 4 polynomials:\(x^2 + x + 1\text{,}\) \(x^2 + x\text{,}\) \(x^2 + 1\text{,}\) and \(x^2\text{.}\)
