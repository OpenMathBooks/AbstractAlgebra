Item 13.3.3.1.c.

To thank your professors for doing such an amazing job all semester, you decide to bake 6 pies. You will give 3 to your favorite abstract algebra professor, 2 to your next favorite professor, and 1 to your third favorite. You know how to make 4 different types of pies. How man different pie-to-professor combinations can you create? Use Burnside's lemma to explain how this relates to the \(X\) and \(G\) in this problem.
