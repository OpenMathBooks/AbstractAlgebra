Consider the two complex numbers \(a+bi\) and \(c+di\text{.}\) We have

\begin{equation*} \phi(a+bi)+\phi(c+di) = a-bi + c - di = a+c - (b+d)i = \phi(a+c+(b+d)i) = \phi((a+bi)+(c+di)) \end{equation*}

and

\begin{equation*} \phi(a+bi)\phi(c+di) = (a-bi)(c-di) = ac + bd - (cb+ad)i = \phi(ac+bd + (cb+ad)i) = \phi((a+bi)(c+di))\text{.} \end{equation*}
